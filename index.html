HANDMADE PIXEL ART
CREATIVITY & RELAXATION

Magic Bean Studio is a creative craft studio making handmade fuse bead and customized pixel designs. Our core mission is “Magic make your bean happen” — offering affordable and meaningful craft experiences to promote emotional wellbeing among students and young people.

Vibrant Colors
Boosts Concentration
Emotional Wellbeing

By following selected pixelated images and color schemes, beads are arranged onto a perforated acrylic plate and heated to set the design. Compared to screen-based digital entertainment, this hands-on experience offers greater relaxation and social interaction.
